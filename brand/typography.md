# QiQ Typography Guide

## Primary Font
**Inter**  
- Clean, modern, highly readable  
- Works well for dashboards and apps  
- System‑UI fallback ensures speed

## Font Weights
- **900** — Logo / Hero titles  
- **700** — Section headers  
- **500** — Body text  
- **400** — Secondary text  

## Usage
- Titles: 28–36px  
- Section headers: 20–24px  
- Body text: 14–16px  
- Buttons: 14–16px, bold  

## Notes
- Avoid serif fonts  
- Keep spacing generous  
- Maintain contrast for accessibility
